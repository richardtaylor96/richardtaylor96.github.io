Here are five ways to calm your crazy mutt:

1. Exercise. If you want a well-behaved dog, you need to exercise him
2. Build a routine. Hyperactivity is often a result of insecurity on the dog's part.
3. Smart toys. Put your dog's brain power to good use. 4. Obedience or trick training. 5. Teach him a new game.
